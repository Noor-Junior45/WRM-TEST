Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2025

Welcome to Noor POS ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application uses your data, specifically regarding your Google account information.

1. Data We Collect

Noor POS is a client-side application that interacts directly with your Google Drive. We do not store your personal data on our own servers. We access the following:

  • Google User Profile: (email address, name, profile picture) to authenticate you and personalize your experience.
  • Google Drive Files: We access only the specific files and folders created by this app (specifically the 'NoorPOS_Data' folder and 'StoreManager_DB' spreadsheet) to store your inventory, sales, and customer data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the purpose of providing the Store Management service:

  • To log you in and maintain your session.
  • To read and write your store data (Products, Sales, Customers) to your personal Google Sheet.
  • To generate invoices and reports based on your data.
Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance:
Noor POS's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Data Sharing and Storage

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

Your data remains stored securely within your own Google Drive account. Our application acts as a tool to view and edit that data. You retain full ownership and control over your files.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since data is stored in your Google Drive, you can delete it at any time by deleting the 'NoorPOS_Data' folder or the 'StoreManager_DB' file in your Google Drive.
